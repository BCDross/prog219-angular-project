Kanban Web Tracker

Overview

This web app is a Kanban or card-based system task tracker. This allows you to quickly see progress on tasks of an ongoing project. As well as maintain more granular data of the

Tasks

The Tasks link above will take you to your tasks list. There you will be able to add, update, and archive or delete the tasks.

Archived Tasks

The Archived Tasks link above will take you to your archived Tasks. Here you can review all of the archived completed tasks.

Sign In

The Sign In link above will allow you to sign into the app. This is a way to save and maintain your tasks. Google SSO integration is currently in development.
